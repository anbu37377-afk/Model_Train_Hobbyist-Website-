About Our Community

Uniting model train enthusiasts since 2026

Who We Are

More Than Just a Hobby

Model Train Hobbyist started as a small forum for locals to share their N-scale layouts. Today, it has grown into a global platform for enthusiasts of all scales and skill levels.

We believe that model railroading is an art form that combines engineering, history, and creativity. Our goal is to make this hobby accessible to everyone.

Behind The Scenes

Where The Magic Happens

Our dedicated studio is where we test every product, film our tutorials, and build our showcase layouts. It's a messy, creative haven filled with sawdust, paint, and passion.

  • State-of-the-art DCC testing bench
  • Dedicated heavy weathering station
  • 3D printing lab for custom parts

Our Mission

To provide high-quality resources, honest reviews, and a supportive community for model train hobbyists worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading digital resource for model railroading, inspiring the next generation of builders.
