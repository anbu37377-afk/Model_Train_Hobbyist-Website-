Comprehensive Planning

Designing a model railroad is complex. You have to consider minimum radii, turnout angles, grades, and aisle width, all while trying to fit in the industries and scenery you want.

Our expert designers use advanced CAD software to create track plans that are operationally interesting and construction-ready.

What You Get:

Full 2D Track Plan in your scale
3D Visualization of the terrain
Wiring Diagram for blocks/DCC
Bill of Materials for track/lumber
